Our Story

Built on a Passion for Exceptional Homes

Founded in 2008, Stackly Estates began with a simple belief: that buying or selling a luxury property should be as refined as the homes themselves. What started as a boutique consultancy in Los Angeles has grown into a global network spanning the world’s most desirable markets.

Our advisors combine deep local knowledge with white-glove service, guiding clients through every stage of the journey—from off-market opportunities and private viewings to negotiation, financing, and closing.
