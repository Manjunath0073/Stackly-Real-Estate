Description

Commanding uninterrupted views of the Pacific Ocean, Cliffside Villa Malibu is a masterclass in contemporary coastal architecture. Floor-to-ceiling glass walls dissolve the boundary between indoor and outdoor living, framing golden sunsets and the rhythmic surf below.

The residence spans three levels of refined finishes, including imported stone, rift-cut white oak, and bespoke bronze detailing. A private elevator, temperature-controlled wine cellar, and smart-home automation provide modern convenience, while expansive terraces, an infinity-edge pool, and an outdoor kitchen create an unrivaled entertaining environment.
